BAKED DONUTS

INGREDIENTS

  • 3/4 all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 5 crushed oreos
  • 6 tablespoons of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted

METHOD

  1. Preheat the oven to 325Â°F
  2. In a bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and Oreos.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the remaining ingredients together.
  4. Stir the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  5. Fill the donut pan with a spoon or using a ziplock bag with a hole cut out of one end.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes or until the top springs back when touched.
